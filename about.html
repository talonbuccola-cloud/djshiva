DJ Shiva Tiffany

With more than two decades behind the decks, DJ Shiva Tiffany has built a sound rooted in house music and elevated by funk, soul, and bass-heavy edge. Based in the San Francisco Bay Area, she has brought that sound to clubs, festivals, and underground events from San Francisco to New York, Miami, and Las Vegas. Her sets are smooth, magnetic, and built to move a room, blending deep groove with high-energy precision.

Performance Profile

Home base: San Francisco Bay Area

Regions performed in: San Francisco, New York, Los Angeles, Miami, Las Vegas, Arizona

Primary genres: House and its subgenres

Additional genres: Breaks, trip hop, trap

Style description: A little bit of funk, a little bit of soul, and a lot of bass.

Event types: Clubs, festivals, underground events

Preferred equipment: CDJ-3000s or Pioneer controllers with plug-and-play

Travel: Available for travel. Rates may vary based on location.

Highlights

Past appearances: Cielo in New York, The Artisan in Las Vegas, Winter Music Conference in Miami, and venues throughout San Francisco.

Artists performed with: Kevin Knapp and Nile.

Every set is shaped to the room first, with house at the center and enough funk, soul, and low-end pressure to keep the energy locked in.
